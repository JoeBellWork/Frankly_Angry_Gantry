Frankly Angry Gantry is a top-down stratergy game where both you and a friend are competing to win employee of the month as a gantry operator. Score points by sorting the cargo containers into their correct loading platform. Get this wrong and you will be fired immediatly. Good luck gamers.

Controls:
Blue Player:
	Move hook up along the arm: W
	Move hook down along the arm: S
	Turn the crane left: A
	Turn the crane right: D
	Drop the hook: Right Space	

Red Player:
	Move hook up along the arm: Up arrow
	Move hook down along the arm: Down arrow
	Turn the crane left: Left Arrow
	Turn the crane right: Right Arrow
	Drop the hook: Right Control
